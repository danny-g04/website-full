My Past

I was born in Lewiston, Idaho. I spent the first five years of my life living with my grandparents in China, and then moved back to the United States to start kindergarden. While other kids were exploring nature and catching bugs at that age, I was running around my family's resturant, messing around, under my parent's watchful eyes. Up until college, that restaurant was the only thing I knew. I was practically running that entire front of house in high school. Unfortunately, due to Covid and staffing, it closed down once I left for college.

My Present

Currently, I am studying at the University of Idaho, majoring in Computer Science. I am also President of the Vandal Table Tennis Club. I first joined the club back in my freshman year, and I had never played before coming to the University. Over the years, I worked my way up and now I would say I'm pretty decent at the sport. Another hobby of mine is collecting Pokemon cards. I started this with my girlfriend, and it's been fun getting rare cards. We have a neat little collection that's worth a bit of money.
